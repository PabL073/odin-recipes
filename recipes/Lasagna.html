Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta that is made from layers of flat pasta sheets, filled with a mixture of meat, vegetables, and cheese. It is then baked in the oven until the cheese melts and the pasta is cooked. Lasagna is a popular dish in Italy and is often served at special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
